Privacy Policy for TapToot

Last updated: April 20, 2026

TapToot is built by RyGar Labs. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

What We Collect

Nothing. TapToot does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no network connection, no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, and no advertising identifiers.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Your selected sound preference
  • Your shuffle preference
  • Audio files you import from the Files app or Voice Memos
  • Audio files you record inside the app

All of this data stays on your iPhone. It is never sent anywhere. It is removed when you delete the app.

Permissions

TapToot requests access to your microphone only when you choose to record a new sound inside the app. Recordings are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted. You can revoke microphone access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

TapToot does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, photos, or any other protected resource. To import an existing recording, TapToot uses the system file picker, which gives the app temporary access only to the single file you choose.

Children

TapToot does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
